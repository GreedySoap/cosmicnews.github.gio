Astro, Demonic, and ReclusePvP

Popular server genre CosmicPvP Test Servers are exploding in poplation and their popularity. Game studio Cosmic Games has had their most popular Minecraft Server CosmicPvP's database leaked to the public, allowing the public to use their custom APIs, and access to their custom Minecraft client Cosmic Client's features to be used on 3rd party, non official servers.

But who wins the popularity vote?

Well the original top dog will win the popularity vote for a while, however the copy servers are catching up slowly. All servers offer the same features as the original such as:

  • Roam
  • Ping Allies
  • Faction Data in Minimaps
  • Voicechat
  • ItemSkins/Custom Blocks

However mainly, the popularity order looks something like this:

  1. CosmicPvP
  2. AstroPvP
  3. DemonicPvP
  4. FantasyCloud

However that doesn't stop the competition from producing better content. Most servers such as AstroPvP, Demonic, and others hire independent developers to modify the leaked plugins. Servers like FantasyCloud don't take advantage of the CosmicClient API, and instead use a playstyle that can be used in vanilla.

But where can you download the plugins?

Websites like InfiniteLeaks, Blackspigot, and other leak websites hold the plugins for download on their websites. Most downloads are somewhat reliable, and most have the same content. However in any world, some are better, more functional, or have more content in their downloads.

Is it illegal?

Well yes and no...

In any world, pirating isnt legal in anyway, and its worse when you're using other's work to profit for yourself. However from a direct statement from Cosmic Game's lead administrator "They'll always exist, and we can't do anything to stop them" - Ypperin. Cosmic isn't taking any action to takedown these servers, so long live CosmicPvP Test Servers?
